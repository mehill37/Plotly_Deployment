Overview of Charts

These charts are used to visualize the bacterial data for the volunteers. All of our charts will adjust to the selected results by choosing the Test Subject ID No. in the dropdown. The bar chart shows us the Top 10 Bacteria Cultures found in the selected subject. The gauge chart shows how the frequency the chosen subject washes their belly button weekly. The bubble chart shows us how many Bacteria Cultures Per Sample we find.
